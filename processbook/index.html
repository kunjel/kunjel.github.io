BID.2008 - Project 4
Research Synthesis Design Solution
 

EyeDog

  

Have you ever wondered how your dog sees the world?

 

Summary

Our assignment was to explore opportunities and design challenges around the idea of an intelligent service cast to support a family. The goal was to design an application of computational intelligence to improve the quality
of people’s lives with respect to a broad definition of family.

We chose to focus to families with dogs. We have designed EyeDog. EyeDog is an intelligent camera which attaches to your dog's collar and records the world from your dog's point of view. This site details our design process and includes a video sketch which demonstrates our final design.
