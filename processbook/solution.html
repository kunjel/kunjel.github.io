Summary

EyeDog is an intelligent camera that attaches to your dog's collar. The camera has image stabilization so it records smooth video even when the dog is moving. It also has motion sensors so it doesn't record when the dog is sleeping.

The device used to control the video output is a picture frame. The picture frame allows the user to watch streaming video or to review videos previously recorded. The user can scan a timeline which breaks up the recorded video and highlights areas with a lot of unusual activity or high amount of barking. The user has the ability to add videos to their favories. Sometimes the picture frame randomly selects a video saved in the favoritres and plays it.
